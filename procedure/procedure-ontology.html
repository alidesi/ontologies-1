Routec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://parliament.uk/ontologies/procedure/Route

Ties a preceding step to a following step depending on applicable procedure, point in time and conditional inputs from the preceding step. Note that routes are time bound (borrowing startOn and endOn from the Core Ontology) allowing for routes to change over time and reflect changes to procedure.

is in range of
